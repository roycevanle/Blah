Collective Mission

"Big 5"

Everyone* shares responsibility to respect, honor, and encourage the backgrounds of all individuals. Everyone shares responsibility to keep the space* safe and confidential for individuals and the community*. Everyone shares responsibility to clean, maintain, and respect the space. We strive to provide services that are guest-centered and empower everyone by interrupting oppression and holding ourselves and each other accountable. We strive to expand access by dismantling barriers and by following a harm reduction approach.

*Who is everyone? “Everyone” includes staff, volunteers, guests, board members, donors, and community partners. When these rules apply? All the time

*What is the space? Our “space” includes the shelter, the church, the alley, and surrounding businesses
